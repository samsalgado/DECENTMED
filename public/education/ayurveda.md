# Ayurveda Experience

Ayurveda, an ancient healing system, offers a holistic approach to wellness by balancing the body, mind, and spirit through natural remedies and personalized treatments. Unlike conventional medicine, Ayurveda focuses on preventing illness rather than just treating symptoms, using herbs, diet, and lifestyle adjustments tailored to each individual's constitution. Patients seeking gentle, long-term healing often turn to Ayurveda for its ability to address root causes rather than temporary relief.

## What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is a holistic healing system developed in India based on the delicate balance between mind, body, and spirit. The goal of Ayurveda is promoting good health instead of fighting disease. The goal is to cleanse your body of undigested food, which stays in your body and can lead to illness. This process is called **Panchakarma**, designed to reduce symptoms and restore harmony and balance.

Other processes used include:

- Blood purification
- Massage
- Medical oils
- Herbs
- Enemas
- Laxatives

## Types of Ayurvedic Oil

1. **Dhanwantharam, Bala oil** – Massage (Abhyanga)  
2. **Bhringraj, Neelibhringadi** – Hair and scalp care  
3. **Mahanarayan oil** – Joint pain relief  
4. **Kumkumadi oil (for glow), Neem oil** – Skin conditions  
5. **Brahmi or Ashwagandha oil** – Stress and sleep
