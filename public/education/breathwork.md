## What is Breathwork?

Breathwork is the practice of consciously using the breath to regulate your physical, emotional, and mental state. By shifting the rhythm, depth, and pattern of your breathing, you can calm the nervous system, release stored tension, increase clarity, and access deeper states of awareness.
