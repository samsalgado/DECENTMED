## 7 Stages of Trauma Healing

### Empowerment for Personal Growth
![Empowerment](empowerment)  
Learn how to tap into your inner strength and resources to achieve personal growth.

---

### Building Resilience
![Resilience](jim2)  
Develop the mental tools necessary to handle adversity with grace and strength.

---

### Finding Balance
![Balance](calm)  
Achieve a healthy work-life balance by prioritizing goals and responsibilities while making time for self-care.

---

### Heal and Empower Yourself
![Success](success)  
Work through trauma to uncover your true self. Gain tools to overcome fear, anxiety, and low self-esteem.

---

### Take Action Toward a Better Future
![Action](jim5)  
Discover actionable steps through reflection and strategy to sustain growth and healing.

---

### Find Community and Build Stronger Relationships
![Support](support)  
Support and accountability to help keep you on track and provide guidance where needed.

---

### Support and Guidance
![Guidance](jim7)  
Identify areas of imbalance and make positive changes that enhance overall well-being.
