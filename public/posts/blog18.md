## 1. Cat-Cow Stretch

Helps with **spinal mobility** by releasing tension from stiffness or prolonged sitting.  

- **Cow:** Arch your back.  
- **Cat:** Tuck your chin and pelvis.  

This dynamic movement improves flexibility in the spine and loosens tight back muscles.

![Cat-Cow Stretch](seatedfold)

---

## 2. Seated Forward Fold

Targets the **hamstrings and lower back**.  

- Sit and lean forward to touch the soles of your feet.  
- Helps counteract tight hamstrings caused by walking or standing in hot weather.  

Combining this stretch with chiropractic adjustments helps release heat-induced muscle tightness.

![Seated Forward Fold](needle)

---

## 3. Thread-the-Needle Stretch

Relieves **tight shoulders and upper back**.  

- Slide your right arm under your left while resting shoulders and head on the floor.  
- Loosens traps and mid-back muscles.  

![Thread-the-Needle Stretch](lying)

---

## 4. Lying Hip Flexor Stretch

Counters **sitting and heat stiffness**.  

- Tight hip flexors are common due to sedentary lifestyles.  
- This stretch improves hip mobility and reduces lower back tension.  

![Lying Hip Flexor Stretch](standing)

---

## 5. Standing Side Stretch

Opens the **ribs and supports cooling through deep breathing**.  

- Stand and stretch to each side.  
- Combine with slow, rhythmic breathing to relax the nervous system.  

This stretch is excellent for summer heat and works best alongside chiropractic adjustments for overall mobility.
