## Conversion Optimization Consulting

A focused conversion optimization strategy can significantly improve how patients and providers interact with your website. Key optimization opportunities include:

- Insert a video on your homepage to immediately explain your value and build trust  
- Link directly to your booking page or add a call button from social media  
- Embed your booking link prominently at the top of each page  
- Create a social media content series that highlights real patient or provider reviews  
- Embed high-volume, low-difficulty keywords into your website content and Google Business Profile description  

These changes help reduce friction, improve engagement, and increase completed bookings without increasing ad spend.
