## What is Batana Oil?

Batana oil is a **buttery substance rich in vitamin E and antioxidants**, extracted from the nuts of the American palm (*Elaeis oleifera*). It is traditionally used by the **Tawira tribe**, known for their healthy, strong hair. According to Dr. Sebi, the enzymes in batana oil penetrate the hair and revive dormant follicles. Applying the oil topically while following a vegetarian or plant-based diet may maximize hair health and growth.
