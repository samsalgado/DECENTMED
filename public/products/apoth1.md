## Abies balsamea
![Image Placeholder](Plants.abiesbalsema)

**Price:** 0.00019  
**Medicinal Rating:** 5/5  
**Source:** [View Details](https://pfaf.org/user/Plant.aspx?LatinName=Abies+balsamea)

**Description:**  
The resin obtained from balsam fir has been used worldwide as an antiseptic and healing agent. Applied externally for burns, wounds, and sores, and internally for coughs and diarrhoea. Widely used by North American tribes for colds, sore throats, and headaches.
