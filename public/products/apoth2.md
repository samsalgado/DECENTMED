## Populus nigra (Black Poplar)
![Image Placeholder](Plants.blackpoplar)

**Price:** 0.000036500248  
**Medicinal Rating:** 3/5  
**Source:** [View Details](https://pfaf.org/User/Plant.aspx?LatinName=Populus+nigra)

**Description:**  
Leaf buds contain salicin, decomposing into salicylic acid (aspirin). Used for bronchitis, stomach disorders, arthritis, and rheumatism. Externally applied for colds, sinusitis, muscular pain, and dry skin conditions.
