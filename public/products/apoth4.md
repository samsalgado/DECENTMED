## Areca catechu (Betel Nut Palm)
![Image Placeholder](Plants.betel)

**Price:** 0.00025952222  
**Medicinal Rating:** 3/5  
**Source:** [View Details](https://pfaf.org/user/Plant.aspx?LatinName=Areca+catechu)

**Description:**  
Seeds chewed as stimulant and digestive aid. Used as an anthelmintic and for intestinal disorders. Excessive use linked to oral cancers due to alkaloid content. Widely cultivated across Asia for cultural and medicinal purposes.
